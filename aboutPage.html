  • Am I a perpetrator

We are a supportive community committed to personal growth and accountability. Our organization provides a safe space for individuals seeking transformation after causing harm. Through mutual understanding, empathy, and shared experiences, we strive to foster positive change, recognizing the potential for redemption and healing in every journey. We are redefining the concept of justice and our mission is to replace traditional punitive measures with innovative, community-driven solutions. We believe in the power of restorative and transformative justice to heal communities, holding perpetrators accountable while fostering positive transformation for a more just and compassionate society.

At Perpetrators Anonymous, our primary focus is on the well-being of individuals navigating the path towards healing and accountability. If you are a survivor of harm and feel that additional support from specialized organizations could enhance your healing process, we encourage you to explore services designed specifically for survivors. These organizations offer valuable resources, including counseling, advocacy, and community services. To assist you in connecting with these recommended organizations, our team is available to provide contact information, answer any questions you may have, and offer support as you take steps towards a healthier and empowered future. Your journey is unique, and we are here to help facilitate the connections that best suit your needs.
