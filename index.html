Welcome to a space dedicated to navigating challenging discussions. Here, we delve into intimate and honest reflections on Intimate Partner Violence (IPV)
Our Mission

Empowering Growth, Fostering Accountability. Our mission at Perpetrators Anonymous is to provide a confidential and supportive space for individuals committed to personal transformation. We strive to empower individuals who have caused harm to recognize their potential for change, promoting a journey towards accountability, healing, and positive growth.

Our Vision

A World Transformed by Accountability and Redemption. Our vision is of a world where individuals who have caused harm find the support and resources needed for genuine transformation. We envision a society that prioritizes rehabilitation over condemnation, fostering understanding, empathy, and collective responsibility for positive change.
